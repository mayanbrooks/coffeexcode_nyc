ABOUT US

COFFEE & CODED is an educational event series for women and girls interested in learning to code! We host monthly events in NYC and the five boroughs. Our team of diverse code professionals are dedicated to creating an engaging learning environment. Join us at our next event! You bring your laptop, we bring the coffee!

OUR MISSION

“We aspire to inspire women and girls to create, code, and challenge assumptions.”

TOPICS INCLUDE:

  • HTML/CSS
  • JAVASCRIPT 101
  • CAREER INSIGHT
