What We Do

COFFEE & CODED is an educational event series for women and girls interested in learning to code! We host monthly events in NYC and the five boroughs. Join us at our next event! You bring your laptop, we bring the coffee!

TOPICS INCLUDE:

  • HTML/CSS
  • JAVASCRIPT 101
  • API INTEGRATION
  • RESUME WORKSHOPS
  • CAREER INSIGHT
  • & MORE
