WHAT'S COFFEE & CODE?

COFFEE & CODE is a collective of tech professionals providing fun and free programming for women and girls interested in learning to code! We host monthly events in NYC and the five boroughs. Our team of diverse code professionals are dedicated to creating an engaging learning environment. Join us at our next event! You bring your brain, we bring the coffee!
