WHY?

 

Because These Stats Byte.

 

Only 20% of Tech Jobs are held by Women.

 

Female Founders receive less than 2% of all VC Funding.

 

Women occupy roughly 11% of executive positions in Silicon Valley.

 

How?

 

Through hands-on, skills-based workshops and community events, we are changing the tide for women in tech. We're proud to empower local engineers, designers, and entrepreneurs as our speakers and instructors, providing a strong model for female leadership.
