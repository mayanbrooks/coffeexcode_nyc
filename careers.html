Careers

We are always looking for talented people to join our team. If you're interested in working with us, check out the positions we have open below. If you don't see a position that fits your skills right now, but still want to be a part of the team, email us at info@coffeeandcoded.com.

Head of Sales & Partnerships

COFFEE & CODED is looking for a dynamic, driven sales & partnerships expert who is as passionate about social impact as they are about building successful relationships. We are a mission-driven organization dedicated to inspiring young women to learn to code and pursue roles in technology. You will be working directly with the Founder.

Our short term goals include:

  • - Developing strategy for brand partnerships/sponsorships
  • - Developing on-campus relationships with local universities
  • - Building and strengthening relationships with other local coding, entrepreneurship, bootcamp, and accelerator organizations
  • - Furthering relationships with industry experts
  • - Sourcing talent for our speaker/educational events
  • *Immediate hire, Part-time
  • *Unpaid (0-5% Equity, open to negotiation)
  • *Local NYC only

This position includes the opportunity to become a formal part of the founding team.

Apply now by sending us an email with your resume attached and a brief intro.
